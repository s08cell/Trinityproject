What is Carbon Footprint?

A carbon footprint is the total amount of greenhouse gases (mainly carbon dioxide, or CO₂) that are emitted into the atmosphere as a result of:

⚫Your activities

⚫The products you use

⚫The services you consume

Examples of Things That Add to Your Carbon Footprint:

🚗 Driving a car (burning petrol/diesel)

✈️ Flying on a plane

⚡ Using electricity from coal or gas-powered plants

🛍️ Buying clothes or electronics

🍔 Eating meat (especially beef/lamb)

🏭 Manufacturing and shipping products

Why It Matters?

Too much carbon in the atmosphere contributes to global warming and climate change — causing rising temperatures, melting glaciers, and extreme weather.
